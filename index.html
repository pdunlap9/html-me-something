Jimmy John's

January 2009 - April 2013

Jimmy John's was my first job, during my last year of High school. It was there that I learned the basics of becoming a valuable employee. I also made many lasting friendships, and I am still close with the general manager.

More Information About Jimmy John's

Jimmy John's is a shop that sells sub sandwiches, they mainly employ college students who do not desire to work full time. They only serve cold sandwiches, and are most known for being 'Freaky Fast.' They deliver, but only within a close radius to the store, to maintain the quickness of service.

My job duties included:

  1. Taking orders quickly, while also being friendly.
  2. Slicing meats and maintaining a certain weight for each portion.
  3. Delivering food quickly, but also safely
  4. Cleaning duties and rotation of product.

Domino's Pizza

January 2012 - August 2017

Domino's provided me with a lot of new skills and responsibilities. I began as a driver, and worked my way to a shift leader, an assistant manager, and eventually a general manager of my own store. I accomplished quite a bit for the company, and was eventually given the busiest store in our area.

More Information About Domino's Pizza

Domino's is a very popular Pizza restaurant. They are constantly updating their menu, and are well known for their low price range, and quick delivery. They also serve salads, subs, pasta, and chicken wings.

My job duties included:

  1. Controlling food costs, and ordering our truck deliveries with enough food to last, but too much so that it would expire before use.
  2. Training new employees, to maintain a steady and uniform workflow.
  3. Establishing a marketing plan for my area, and keeping sales above the previous year's to date.
  4. Forming relationships with repeat customers.
  5. Counting the money each night, setting alarms, and locking the doors.

Bogart's Smokehouse

April 2017 - September 2020

Bogart's was a fun and laid back place to work. All of the employees and owners were like my family. It was important to make every customer feel comfortable and have an easy experience. It was a very small building and we were all very close knit. I became a shift leader pretty quickly after I started there.

More Information About Bogart's Smokehouse

Bogart's is a tiny restaurant owned by the same owners of the more wellknown Pappy's Smokehouse. While the menu is similar, Bogart's food is quite different. Due to the smaller amount of clientelle each day, they are able to take their time, and make smaller batches of food each day. Even though the building is small, and the lines are long do not be decieved. Service is quick and friendly.

My job duties included:

  1. Checking each order for correctness, and running them to tables.
  2. Keeping an eye on the dining room, and helping customers with any questions or concerns.
  3. Assigning job duties, and ensuring everyone stayed on task.
  4. Controlling the flow of traffic into the bulding by holding the line outside, and having conversations with customers as they were waiting.

TD Ameritrade

September 2020 - Current

I have just begun my job at TD Ameritrade. Currently I am studying to pass my SIE and series 7 exams. I am currently working from home due to the pandemic, and almost everything about this job is new to me. It is exciting to be developing a new skill set, although very difficult. This is also my first job orking overnights.

More Information About TD Ameritrade

TD Ameritrade is dedicated to providing first class service. They offer many services for free, and have a wide array of free education items on their website. They were rated #1 overall broker on stockbrokers.com

My job duties include:

  1. In order to keep the job, I must pass these exams.
  2. Answering phones and helping customers with their needs or connecting them to someone who can.
  3. Learning new systems, and how to read stock quotes.
  4. Ensuring I am maintaining the security of each and every account.

I am excited for what the future holds as far as my professional life. Im hoping to maintain my current employment while possibly working part time during the week with my new coding skillset. My goals are to have a wide array of skills, and to enjoy whatever job I choose to do.
